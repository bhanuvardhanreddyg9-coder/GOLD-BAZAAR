Platform Flow

How a deal happens — step by step

Four steps. Honest about the time and effort each takes.

01

Create & Verify

Sign up with your phone number. OTP login. Complete KYC (Aadhaar or PAN). Without KYC you can browse, but you cannot post or transact. KYC takes 1–2 business days.

02

Post Your Gold

Add weight, karat, item type, hallmark number, photos, and your asking price. Set a private minimum you'll accept. Your listing is visible to all users immediately.

03

Negotiate & Agree

Buyers send offers. You chat on the platform. Agree on a price. You don't have to accept the first offer. This part can take a few days depending on demand.

04

Escrow & Handover

Buyer pays into escrow. You ship gold (insured courier) or meet in person. Buyer confirms receipt. GoldSwap deducts 1–5% commission and releases the balance to your bank account.

User Guide

How to use GoldSwap — plain steps

No jargon. No hype. Just what to do and what to expect.

01

Create your account

Go to Login. Enter your mobile number. You receive a 6-digit OTP by SMS. Enter it and your account is created. No password is stored. Same login works on mobile app and website.

02

Complete KYC (mandatory before transacting)

Upload Aadhaar or PAN. A licensed KYC provider verifies it. Takes 1–2 business days. Until then you can browse but not post or buy.

03

Check today's gold rate

The ticker at the top shows today's market rate. Use it as a reference when you set your asking price. GoldSwap does not guarantee these rates — they are pulled from MCX and may lag by a few minutes.

04

To sell: Post your item

Fill in all fields — weight, karat, type, hallmark, photos, price. Your listing goes live immediately. Edit or remove it any time before a deal is confirmed.

05

To buy: Browse and make an offer

Filter by karat, city, price. Click "Contact Seller." Chat. Make an offer. The seller can accept, counter, or ignore it. There is no guarantee anyone responds quickly.

06

Lock the deal — escrow payment

When both sides agree, the buyer pays into GoldSwap's escrow (via Razorpay or similar). The money does not go to the seller yet. This protects both parties.

07

Handover the gold

Meet in person or ship via insured courier. Log the courier tracking number on the platform. GoldSwap does not arrange pickup — that is between you and the other person.

08

Buyer confirms — you get paid

Buyer confirms receipt. GoldSwap deducts commission and releases balance to your bank account within 2–3 business days. If buyer doesn't confirm or raise a dispute within 48 hours of delivery, funds release automatically.

09

Disputes

If there's a problem (wrong purity, damaged item), raise a dispute within 48 hours of confirmed delivery. GoldSwap staff review. They may request a third-party assay test. Resolution takes 5–10 business days. The platform decision is final.

⚠️ Things to know before you start

GoldSwap is not a jeweller, bank, or commodity exchange. It is a marketplace that charges commission.

Deals can fall through. Buyers can back out before paying into escrow. Sellers can withdraw listings. This is normal and not covered by any guarantee.

GoldSwap cannot verify purity claims made by sellers. Always do your own verification — especially for high-value items. Get a hallmark test if in doubt.

Tax on gold sales may apply. Consult a tax advisor if you are selling regularly or in large amounts.

Technical Specification

Backend Design Plan

For developers. Covers APIs, data models, auth, and transaction workflow.

⚠️ Read this before you hire a developer

  • Legal: Peer-to-peer gold trading may require an RBI licence or state-level registration depending on volume and structure. Check before launch.
  • Escrow: Holding buyer money until delivery requires a payment gateway with escrow/route-split capability (Razorpay, Cashfree). A normal payment link does not do this.
  • KYC: Aadhaar/PAN verification requires integration with a licensed KYC API provider (Digio, SignDesk, Hyperverge). It is not free.
  • Dispute resolution: Software can log a dispute. A human being must actually resolve it. Budget for staff time.
  • Timeline: A working MVP of this platform will take 4–8 months with a small team, not weeks. Do not let anyone tell you otherwise.

1. Authentication

Phone-number OTP only. No passwords stored. JWT sessions.

Step What the system does
User enters phone Server generates OTP, stores it hashed with 5-minute expiry. Sends via SMS gateway (MSG91 or AWS SNS).
User enters OTP Server compares hash. If valid, issues access_token (15-min expiry) + refresh_token (30-day expiry). Both are signed JWTs.
API requests Client sends Authorization: Bearer <token>. Server validates signature and expiry on every request.
KYC gate All POST / transaction endpoints return 403 KYC_REQUIRED until the user's KYC status is verified.
Cross-platform Same token works on web and mobile app. Logout invalidates all tokens for that user.

4. Commission Logic

Always calculated server-side. Never trust the client.

  • Default range: 1% to 5%. Rate is set by platform admin per listing category or deal size.
  • Commission is charged to the seller — deducted before payout.
  • Formula: net_to_seller = agreed_price × (1 − commission_pct / 100)
  • Example: ₹85,000 at 2% → ₹83,300 to seller, ₹1,700 to GoldSwap.
  • Commission rate is shown to seller before they accept any deal. No surprises.
  • GST on the commission amount must be calculated separately under current tax rules. Get a CA to confirm.
  • No commission is charged on refunded or cancelled transactions.

5. Escrow & Payment Flow

Money does not reach the seller until the buyer confirms. This requires a real escrow setup — not a basic payment link.

  • Step 1: Buyer pays via /transactions/:id/pay. Money goes into payment gateway escrow (Razorpay or Cashfree route-split). It does not touch GoldSwap's main account.
  • Step 2: Seller ships gold. Logs tracking number on platform via /transactions/:id/ship.
  • Step 3: Buyer confirms receipt via /transactions/:id/confirm. This triggers automatic payout: commission to GoldSwap, balance to seller's bank account.
  • Auto-release: If buyer takes no action within 48 hours of logged delivery, funds auto-release to seller.
  • Dispute hold: Dispute raised → all funds frozen until admin resolves. Can take 5–10 business days.
  • Refund: If dispute resolved in buyer's favour → full refund. No commission charged.
  • Payout timeline: Funds reach seller's bank within 2–3 business days after release.

6. Daily Gold Rate Feed

Displayed rates are for reference only. They are not prices GoldSwap guarantees.

  • A scheduled background job runs daily at 10:00 AM IST, fetching rates from MCX API or a licensed data vendor.
  • Rates stored in gold_rates table by karat.
  • GET /gold-rate/today returns the most recent record with its fetched_at timestamp.
  • If the feed fails, the previous day's rate is served with a visible "Rate may be outdated" warning in the UI.
  • GoldSwap is not a commodity exchange. The rate displayed does not bind any transaction.
